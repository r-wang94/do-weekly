# Reading List

Books, papers, studies, and articles worth referencing or drawing from. A growing bibliography for the scriptwriter.

---

## Books

| Book | Author | Key ideas for Do Weekly |
|---|---|---|
| *Atomic Habits* | James Clear | Identity-based change, habit stacking, the votes metaphor, environment design |
| *Tiny Habits* | B.J. Fogg | B=MAP model, celebration as the habit-forming mechanism, anchoring to existing routines |
| *The Power of Now* | Eckhart Tolle | Present-moment awareness, the thinking mind as the source of suffering, "the next moment is never more important than this one" |
| *Why We Sleep* | Matthew Walker | Sleep as master regulator, ghrelin/leptin dysregulation, sleep debt, amygdala reactivity |
| *The Circadian Code* | Satchin Panda | Time-restricted feeding, melatonin and insulin, eating windows, chronobiology |
| *Feel Better in 5* | Rangan Chatterjee | Health snacks, 5-minute interventions, the ripple effect across pillars |
| *Happy Mind, Happy Life* | Rangan Chatterjee | Core Happiness (Alignment, Control, Contentment), the Want Brain, Junk Happiness |
| *The 4 Pillar Plan* | Rangan Chatterjee | The interconnected 4-pillar framework, root causes, the threshold effect |
| *Flow* | Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi | Flow state, the ~4% stretch principle, intrinsic motivation, the antidote to the Want Brain |
| *The Blue Zones* | Dan Buettner | Longevity through environment and culture, natural movement, plant-heavy diet, purpose |
| *The Stress Prescription* | Elissa Epel | Allostatic load, stress as cumulative wear, the body's stress response systems |
