# Quotes

A timeless bank of quotes, phrases, and lines organised by theme. These are raw material for any episode — not tied to a specific script. If a direct quote has been spoken verbatim in an episode, it's tagged so it isn't repeated word-for-word.

---

- "We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit." — Will Durant (commonly attributed to Aristotle)
- "The secret of change is to focus all of your energy not on fighting the old, but on building the new." — Socrates (via Dan Millman)
- "First we make our habits, then our habits make us." — Charles Dryden
- "People do not decide their futures. They decide their habits, and their habits decide their futures." — F.M. Alexander
- "You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems." — James Clear
